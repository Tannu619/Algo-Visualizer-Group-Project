About

The “Algorithm Visualizer” software is a tool for students and
teachers at a large scale to visualize and understand different
algorithms and data structures. It is a platform to read, write,
visualize and understand the difficult things of data structures
and algorithms. It is a very interactive platform to ask your
questions and queries. The application enables users to
visualize the thing using some videos or photos, to get detailed notes,visualize
graphs variations, algorithm analysis.
